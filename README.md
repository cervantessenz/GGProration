# Proration Calculator

A simple web-based calculator for determining prorated rent and pet rent for move-ins and move-outs.

---

## 📌 Overview

This tool is designed to quickly and accurately calculate prorated rent amounts based on move-in and move-out dates. It eliminates the need for manual calculations and reduces errors during leasing workflows.

The calculator follows a standard 30-day proration method adjusted to our company policy.

---

## ⚙️ Features

### Move-In Proration
- Calculates prorated rent based on move-in date
- Includes optional pet rent
- Automatically determines remaining days in the month
- Displays next full payment due date

### Move-Out Proration
- Calculates prorated rent based on move-out date
- Includes optional pet rent
- Displays:
  - Payment due date (1st of the month)
  - Late date (5th of the month)

---

## 🧮 Calculation Method

- Uses a **30-day fixed month standard**
- Daily rate = Monthly Rent ÷ 30
- Move-in:
  - Charges for remaining days in the month
- Move-out:
  - Charges for days occupied in the month

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser  
2. Enter:
   - Move-in or move-out date  
   - Monthly rent  
   - Pet rent (optional)  
3. Click **Calculate**  
4. View prorated totals and payment dates instantly
